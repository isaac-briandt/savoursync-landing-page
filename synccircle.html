Three Weekly Anchors. Four Ways to Earn.

Driving intentional cultural activation across every key demographic

Every Thursday Night 🎤 12 PTS

After-Work Karaoke

Target Segment: Urban professionals, corporate friend groups (Ages 25-38).

Strategic Value: Captures the unofficial start of Ghana's social weekend. Highly interactive, shareable, and builds deep digital social proof.

Digital Touchpoints: Wednesday IG teaser, Thursday AM WhatsApp alert framework, plus automated UGC video check-in bonus features (+5 pts).

Mechanic: 12 pts/visit base. Maintain a 3-week attendance streak to earn a free drink token. Insiders gain priority booth booking rights.

Every Saturday Peak 🧸 12+ PTS

The Kids Corner

Target Segment: Young families and parents with children (Ages 28-42).

Strategic Value: Family dining options remain deeply underserved in Accra and Takoradi. This serves as SavourSync’s strongest structural differentiator.

Digital Touchpoints: Saturday Facebook & Instagram family narratives, integrated WhatsApp 'FAMILY' fast-booking flow structures.

Mechanic: 12 pts base + 5 pts multiplier per child in attendance. Streak incentive: 3 Saturdays triggers a 'Kids Eat Free' voucher reward.

Every Sunday Continuous 👑 15 PTS

Buffet & Live Band

Target Segment: Extended family circles, premium brunch crowds, and weekly ritual diners (Ages 25-50).

Strategic Value: The Sunday dining ritual is the hardest consumer habit to break. Combining an absolute buffet with live sound yields maximum perceived value.

Digital Touchpoints: Friday preview blast email, Saturday evening teaser Reel, and Sunday morning 8AM sharp WhatsApp broadcast alerts.

Mechanic: 15 points base (Highest standalone assignment). Regulars+ gain priority tables; VIPs unlock standing baseline reservations.
